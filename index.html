Balance Transfer Guide · CashEntry.in
Balance Transfer Se ₹2 Lakh Tak Kaise Bachaayein
📅 2026⏱️ 5 min

Kya aap abhi bhi ek aise loan ki EMI bhar rahe hain jiska interest rate 16% ya usse zyada hai? Toh aap har mahine hazaron rupaye zyada de rahe hain.

💡 Quick Fact: ₹5 Lakh ka loan 18% se 11% par transfer karo — 3 saal mein ₹1.2 Lakh bacha sakte hain!

Balance Transfer Kya Hota Hai?

Balance Transfer matlab hai — apna existing loan ek bank se doosre bank mein lower interest rate par shift karna.

Balance Transfer Ke 5 Bade Fayde

  • EMI Kam Hoti Hai: Lower rate = lower monthly EMI.
  • Total Interest Bachta Hai: Puri tenure mein lakhs ki savings.
  • Top-Up Loan Option: Extra loan bhi le sakte ho.
  • Tenure Adjust: Chaho toh tenure badhaao.
  • Better Service: Purani bank chhodke switch karo!

Kab Karna Chahiye?

✅ Transfer karo agar:
• Current aur new rate mein 2%+ fark ho
• Remaining tenure 12+ months ho
• CIBIL score 700+ ho

Real Example

📊 ₹3L | 18% → 11% | 24 months
Old EMI: ₹14,975 → New EMI: ₹13,876
Monthly Saving: ₹1,099 | Total: ₹26,376!

Flexi Loan Guide · CashEntry.in
Flexi Loan Kya Hai? Personal Loan Se Kaise Alag
📅 2026⏱️ 6 min

₹5 Lakh ki limit mili. Jo chahein nikalo, jab chahein wapas karo — interest sirf nikale hue amount par.

⚡ Key: Flexi Loan mein PURI amount ka interest nahi — sirf use kiye hue amount ka!

Flexi vs Personal — Comparison

📊 ₹5L zaroorat:
Personal: ₹5L liya → ₹5L par interest
Flexi: ₹5L limit → ₹2L nikale → Sirf ₹2L par interest → Wapas → Limit phir ₹5L!

6 Bade Fayde

  • Sirf use par interest — ₹1L nikala → sirf ₹1L par!
  • Kabhi bhi withdraw karo — emergency mein instant
  • Kabhi bhi wapas karo — no penalty
  • Revolving credit — wapas karo → limit phir available
  • No fixed EMI — sirf interest pay karo
  • Irregular income waalon ke liye best!
